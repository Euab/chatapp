About ChatApp.

ChatApp is just a place to chill with your friends. You may be telling yourself, "This looks terrible. I might aswell use Skype or Discord.". Well, it is terrible. That's what makes it great. The developers of this project are both thirteen, and would like someone to actually use this crap. Anyway, you can send messages to your peepz, and also change your online status if there is someone you are avoiding. Trust us, we know.

Desktop Version.

At the moment, our main priority is the web version. But, we do have plans to create a Desktop App version of ChatApp, that will hopefully be compatible with Windows, Mac OS, and Linux (we are Linux users ourselves).

Mobile Version.

At the moment, as I said before, our main priority is the web version. Then, it's the Desktop Version. After that, if we have gained enough users to keep us going, we will start working on the mobile version, compatible with iOS, and Android. But until then, there will most likely only be PC compatible versions, however the website is mobile-friendly.
